About Genoma Games

Founded in 2015, we're a team of passionate gamers creating experiences

we've always wanted to play.

Our Story

Nexus Studios began with five friends sharing a small apartment and a big dream: to create games that would push the boundaries of the medium.

 

What started as late-night brainstorming sessions has grown into a vibrant studio with over 50 talented developers.

 

Our journey hasn't been without challenges, but our commitment to innovation and player-centric design has allowed us to create award-winning experiences that resonate with gamers worldwide.

 

With each project, we aim to push our creative boundaries further and deliver experiences that stay with players long after they've put down the controller.

Player-Centering Design
We create games with players at the center of every decision, ensuring engaging and memorable experiences.
Cutting-Edge Technology
Our in-house engine pushes the boundaries of what's possible, delivering stunning visuals and innovative gameplay.
Diverse Perspectives
Our international team brings unique viewpoints to our creative process, enriching our storytelling and world-building.
Creative Freedom
We empower our teams to take creative risks and explore new ideas, leading to bold and original gaming experiences.
